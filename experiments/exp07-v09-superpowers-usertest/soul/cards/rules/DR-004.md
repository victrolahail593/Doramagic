title: "Subagent 必须用 fresh instance"
severity: HIGH
rule: |
  Each subagent task must use a fresh subagent instance to avoid context pollution.
  Reusing the same subagent leads to accumulated context and degraded quality.
do:
  - "Dispatch a new subagent for each independent task"
  - "Use implementer-prompt.md as template for fresh subagent"
dont:
  - "Reuse subagent across multiple tasks"
  - "Carry over context between tasks"
confidence: 1.0

  - "skills/subagent-driven-development/SKILL.md:L10-20"

## 真实场景
你用同一个 subagent 连续执行了 5 个任务。随着任务增加，subagent 的输出质量越来越差——它开始重复代码、忘记检查某些条件、提交信息也不规范了。

## 影响范围
- 谁会遇到：使用 subagent-driven-development 的开发者
- 什么时候：连续执行多个任务时
- 多严重：上下文污染导致质量逐次下降
